Musical Synopsis [cite: 37]

The world turns on its head for Sorority president Elle Woods when her long-term boyfriend, Warner, dumps her with plans on moving from UCLA to Harvard to study law[cite: 38, 39]. He tells Elle he needs someone serious, and leaves her in the dust[cite: 39, 40].

Determined to get the love of her life back, Elle works to get into Harvard Law herself, resolved on proving to Warner she can be exactly who he needs her to be[cite: 40, 41].

"What, like it's hard?"

Once in Boston, Elle faces roadblocks she has never had to face before - she learns quickly that no-one seems to take her seriously thanks to her bright persona and bubbly personality[cite: 42].

With the encouragement of her new-found friend and owner of the local salon, Paulette, and the support of Harvard graduate Emmett, Elle works harder than ever to prove to everyone - not just Warner - that she belongs at Harvard just as much as every other student[cite: 43, 44].

Full of astounding dance numbers and songs you'll be singing the whole way home. Legally Blonde is a story about self-worth, independence, and believing in yourself and your own abilities to do whatever you set your mind to[cite: 45, 46].

Frequently Asked Questions

Why do I need a guardian form?
This form is essential for ensuring that we have all necessary contact and medical information for each cast member. Your prompt attention helps us ensure a smooth rehearsal process[cite: 34, 35].
What should I wear to rehearsals?
Please wear clothing that is comfortable and allows for easy movement (e.g. active wear or theatre blacks) to ensure comfort and ease of movement when learning choreography[cite: 32].
Do I have to learn the ensemble track?
Yes. Everyone must learn the ensemble track alongside the excerpts for any lead/supporting character you plan to audition for[cite: 13].
What if I don't get a callback?
If you do not receive a call-back, this does not mean you have not been cast in the production. Call-backs are for lead and supporting roles only[cite: 15, 16].
