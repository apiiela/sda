Musical Synopsis

The world turns on its head for Sorority president Elle Woods when her long-term boyfriend, Warner, dumps her with plans on moving from UCLA to Harvard to study law. He tells Elle he needs someone serious, and leaves her in the dust.

Determined to get the love of her life back, Elle works to get into Harvard Law herself, resolved on proving to Warner she can be exactly who he needs her to be.

"What, like it's hard?"

Once in Boston, Elle faces roadblocks she has never had to face before - she learns quickly that no-one seems to take her seriously thanks to her bright persona and bubbly personality.

With the encouragement of her new-found friend and owner of the local salon, Paulette, and the support of Harvard graduate Emmett, Elle works harder than ever to prove to everyone - not just Warner - that she belongs at Harvard just as much as every other student.

Full of astounding dance numbers and songs you'll be singing the whole way home. Legally Blonde is a story about self-worth, independence, and believing in yourself and your own abilities to do whatever you set your mind to.

Characters

Full breakdowns and vocal requirements for all roles.

Elle Woods

F#2/Gb2 - G5

Elle appears like a typical blonde California sorority girl, majoring in fashion merchandising and standing president of her sorority Delta Nu. She is hardworking, optimistic and tenacious.

Must be comfortable performing in character heels.

Emmett Forrest

B2 - A4

Emmett has had to work hard his entire life to get into Harvard Law School and nothing is going to come between him and becoming a partner in a law firm someday. He is determined not to let Elle waste the incredible opportunity she's been given.

Warner Huntington III

D#3/Eb3 - G#4/Ab4

Elle's ex boyfriend who dumps her with the intention of moving forward with his life at Harvard Law as someone to be taken much more seriously. Warner is driven by his own self-motivation to be as good as his older brother a Yale Law graduate.

Paulette Bonafonte

A3 - A5

The brash yet caring hairdresser at the Hair Affair who becomes quick friends with Elle when she moves to Boston. Paulette's honest and audacious personality makes her a fun and comical character on stage.

Vivienne Kensington

A3 - F5

Warner's highschool girlfriend who he re-kindles with when starting his new life at Harvard. Dry, quick-witted and wickedly smart. Vivienne dismisses Elle immediately due to her bright and bubbly exterior.

Professor Callahan

Does not sing solo

The stern and rather pompous professor of criminal law at Harvard Law School. Like her classmates, Callahan dismisses Elle due to her attitude early on, but eventually invites her on as an intern on his latest murder trial.

Brooke Wyndham

A3 - G5

Former sorority sister of Delta Nu and worldwide fitness influencer, who has been accused on murdering her twice-her-age husband and hires Callahan as her lawyer. Brooke has an alibi that could save her from a lifetime of prison, but sharing her story could ruin everything she has built for herself.

Pilar, Margot & Serena

F#3/Gb3 - C#5/Db5

Elle's closest friends in her Sorority Delta Nu - perform as a trio and lead the Delta Nu ensemble. The girls are bold, flashy and utterly without shame and support Elle in her endeavours to move across the country.

Kiki & The Salon Workers

D4 - A4

Paulette's co-workers at the Hair Affair. Brash and bubbly as Paulette herself, but with more confidence. Kiki leads the ensemble in performing Bend and Snap.

Main Ensemble

All Ranges

Will consist of Harvard students and staff, members of the court, etc. Minor roles to be cast include Kyle the mailman, Chutney Wyndham. Expected to learn all group songs (even if they are not on stage for the number).

Featured Dancers

All Ranges

An Ensemble made up of very strong Dancers. One or Two dancers will also be considered for the Dance Captain position. Expected to learn all group songs.

Frequently Asked Questions

Why do I need a guardian form?
This form is essential for ensuring that we have all necessary contact and medical information for each cast member. Your prompt attention helps us ensure a smooth rehearsal process.
What should I wear to rehearsals?
Please wear clothing that is comfortable and allows for easy movement (e.g. active wear or theatre blacks) to ensure comfort and ease of movement when learning choreography.
Do I have to learn the ensemble track?
Yes. Everyone must learn the ensemble track alongside the excerpts for any lead/supporting character you plan to audition for.
What if I don't get a callback?
If you do not receive a call-back, this does not mean you have not been cast in the production. Call-backs are for lead and supporting roles only.
